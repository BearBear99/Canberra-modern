Est. 2016 · Canberra, ACT

Celebrating the mid-century capital.

Canberra Modern is a volunteer-led organisation dedicated to raising awareness of Canberra's mid-century modern architecture and landscape. Through tours, talks and public programs, we connect the community with the city's built environment and champion its ongoing conservation.
